About Digital and Corporate Innovation

The Digital and Corporate Innovation (SX) Team was approved by the Board to strengthen digital services and systems with the aim to transform and drive Bentley into a digital future.

The department serves to identify new opportunities within the company, to manage a digital portfolio of projects, and implement methods that enable cross-department collaboration. The team is made up of 21 members ranging of a broad variety of expertise in order to deliver necessary projects in-house. These areas include; Digital, Cyber Security, Business and Data Analytics, Software Development, Innovation, Collaboration and Customer Centricity.

With this expertise SX supports all other departments with pilot funding, partners, and resources. They clarify challenges, identify solution to plan and deliver pilots. By having central ownership of common enablers and capabilities they can secure critical mass and leverage on Bentley scale.
